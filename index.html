How Neutron Approaches Documents

Precise identification of valuable data within the document for extraction is the key to efficient document parsing. The data comes in forms of key-value pair (Ex: Invoice Number: 34332) or line items (Ex: A table of material, quantity, etc.) Neutron precisely extracts such data. When a certain situation requires a review of the extracted data, Neutron's mechanism of “human review” for data limits the level of error while maximizing automated document parsing.

Neutron is resilient

In simple terms, Neutron is based on the foundations of flexible template extraction, thereby allowing data extraction even though the position of the fields has changed. You don’t need to rack your brains over each new document with a tolerable level of position changes to extract data.
  Neutron is also equipped to handle common variations in the field names on the form. So, Invoice Number or Inv No. – Neutron is intelligent enough to know they’re the same.

Rides over anything thrown at it

Neutron has a competitive edge while dealing with the most complex structures, be it multiple tables with variable column data, a sub-table within a table, or a continued table over multiple pages. The data is extracted, and the output is structured in just the way you expect.

While these unforeseen challenges might stump most invoice capture solutions, Neutron’s powerful engine can isolate the different structures and extract the necessary information without a sweat.

Functional extension comes naturally

An intelligent document processing platform must provide due support for operational automation at the pre and post stages of actual data extraction. (Ex: An Invoice PDF extraction requires the Invoice to be pulled up from within the Network drives or from the ERP). Neutron is no different, and it has wide choices ranging from API, and webhooks to extending your data entry through BOTs. Neutron is equipped to automate the stages of pulling business documents periodically from within your systems, doing the actual extraction, and then updating the data from extracted documents to another system - all without involving any new systems. Even if your application cannot support API, Neutron can work with BOTs from leading RPA providers to perform similar operations to support the complete life cycle of your valued data.

The genomic sequence

Neutron’s data extraction process runs through a series of steps that starts with identifying the document type to relate to using previous learning of data parsing with similar documents (Ex: A new invoice PDF is extracted based on successful extraction and acceptance from past invoices). It then aligns, cleans, and enhances the visuals so that OCR has better accuracy to identify content and then scans to understand the document scene. Neutron gets to the job of document parsing before presenting the extracted output in various forms.
