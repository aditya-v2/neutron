Less than a minute spent for each invoice 

3X faster data entry to SAP system, Near zero errors

• A manufacturing firm had allocated a team to input its 1,200+ monthly invoices into SAP.
• The varied formats of invoices generated by multiple vendors only added to the complexity of the task.
• As critical as this process was, it was time-consuming, error-riddled, and expensive. 

Solution

• Neutron was trained using multiple invoice formats as provided by the customer.

• Neutron then began extracting digital data from the invoices. 

 

• With increasing volumes, data extraction accuracy grew, and by the 6-month mark, the client saw 88% accuracy.
• This led to phase 2 of the project where we completely automated their data entry process into SAP via BOTs. 

The Benefits

Within months of deploying Neutron and RPA, the customer was able to slash their costs and boost their process efficiency.

Document variations handled seamlessly

$2500 worth of human effort saved every month

• A major Shipping company spent thousands of dollars on data entry for their Bills of Lading.

• Format variability and excessive content (at least 5 text fields and multiple tables) made this process tedious.

• With each Bill of Lading requiring 5,000+ keystrokes, data accuracy took a hit. And even with a (manual) maker checker, accuracy continued to remain a pain point.  

Solution

• We trained Neutron using samples of the various formats and then initiated the data extraction process.

• With ML at its core, Neutron learned and got better.
  

 

• 6 months post-deployment, the client saw 90% accuracy in data identification and extraction.

The Benefits

With key resources now focused on revenue-generating activities along with significantly faster processing, the client enjoyed a substantial positive impact on their bottom line.
